Mobile Forensics

Technical walkthroughs through mobile forensics, for both android and iphones! For JHU class Digital Forensics!
Some muted text

Mobile Forensics is vitally important as digital crime increases!
With the current state of mobile devices and the advancements and capabilities being more than what they used to be; more people are putting more of their lives on their mobile devices. They are trusting their phones with:

  • Bank Information
  • Usernames/Passwords
  • Daily Habits/Personal Assistants/Routines/Alarms
  • Social Media Accounts
  • Multiple photos/videos/recordings
  • Contacts/Voicemail/Video Chats
  • Text Messages/Third-Party Messaging Apps
  • GPS Information
  • Multiple Third-Party Applications
  • Medical Information
  • Now your COVID vaccination information!!
